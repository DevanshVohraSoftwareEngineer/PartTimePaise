**Tinder for Tasks - Swipe, Match, Earn!**

**Core Features:**
- **Swipe-based matching** - Intuitive UX familiar to Gen Z
- **Stranger-to-stranger connections** - Breaking barriers between people
- **Real-time chat** - Instant communication between matched pairs
- **Escrow payments** - Secure transactions with Razorpay integration
